MiniPrediction

MiniPrediction is a game inspired by prediction markets like Polymarket. We think prediction markets are cool, but we’re curious who would win in a poll where you can vote as many times as you’d like - you vote with your efforts instead of your dollars.

You can vote one time, you can vote many times, you can organize campaigns and get your friends to vote, but regardless of how things pan out, one candidate will emerge the winner.

There are no rules, have fun!
